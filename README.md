# Daily Planner

A free daily planner that lives in your browser. Organise your tasks, schedule your day, and take notes — all in one clean, simple app. No account needed, no data leaves your computer.

---

## What You Get

### Tasks
Add anything you need to do, give it a priority, and check it off when it's done. High priority tasks show a red dot, medium shows orange, and low shows green so you can see what matters most at a glance.

### Schedule
Plan your day hour by hour from 6 AM to 10 PM. Click any time slot and type what you're doing — meetings, breaks, deep work, whatever fits your routine.

### Notes
Jot down anything else that doesn't fit into tasks or time blocks — ideas, reminders, phone numbers, quick thoughts.

### Date Navigation
Move forward or backward through days with the arrow buttons. Hit **Today** to jump straight back to the current date. Every day stores its own data independently.

### Dark Mode
Click the moon icon in the top right corner of the header to switch between light and dark themes. Your choice is remembered.

## Getting Started

### Option 1: Just Open the File

1. Download this repository or clone it
2. Double-click `index.html`
3. It opens in your browser and works immediately

No installation, no build step, no server required. The whole app is three files.

### Option 2: Install as a Desktop App

1. Open `index.html` in **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge**
2. Click the three-dot menu in the top right corner
3. Go to **Apps** then **Install Daily Planner**
4. The app appears in your Start Menu and Taskbar like any other program

### Option 3: Add to Your Phone

1. Open the app in your phone's browser
2. Tap the **Share** button
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. The app opens full-screen when you tap the icon on your home screen

## How to Use Each Feature

**Adding a task:** Type in the box, pick a priority from the dropdown, and press the **+** button or hit Enter.

**Completing a task:** Click the checkbox next to it. The text gets crossed out and the counter updates.

**Deleting a task:** Hover over the task and click the **x** that appears on the right.

**Scheduling your day:** Click any time slot in the Schedule panel and type what's happening at that hour.

**Writing notes:** Click into the Notes box and start typing. A counter at the top right shows how many characters you've used out of 1000.

**Changing the date:** Use the **<** and **>** arrows to move between days. Each day keeps its own tasks, schedule, and notes.

**Switching themes:** Click the moon/sun icon in the header to toggle dark mode on and off.

---

## Where Your Data Lives

Everything is stored in your browser's **local storage** — a built-in feature that keeps data on your computer without needing a server. Each day gets its own storage slot based on the date.

**Your data never leaves your device.** There are no accounts, no cloud sync, no tracking.

---

## Warning: Do Not Clear Your Browser Cache

Because all your planner data lives in your browser, clearing your cache or site data **will permanently delete everything** — every task, every schedule entry, every note across all days.

To avoid losing your data:

- **Do not** use "Clear browsing data" with the cached images and files option checked
- **Do not** use incognito or private browsing windows for daily planning (data does not persist)
- **Do not** use browser extensions that auto-clear storage or cookies
- If you need to clear your browser data, make sure to **exclude this site** or back up first

Your data is safe as long as you leave your browser storage alone.
